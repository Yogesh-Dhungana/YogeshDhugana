A little about me

I am a fourth-year bachelor's student at New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD), where I am studying Economics and Social Research & Public Policy. My passions lie in education, leadership, and community engagement. Growing up in a rural village in Far-west Nepal with limited opportunities has instilled in me a strong sense of giving back to the community. I enjoy participating in activities that help to minimize the opportunity gap and empower young minds.
